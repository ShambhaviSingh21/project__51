Interesting Facts about Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  • Their eyes are different to normal bears
  • The giant panda’s scientific name is Ailuropoda melanoleuca, which means “black and white cat-foot”.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • They mainly change color in order to communicate or regulate body temperature
  • Their eyes can swivel around in two different directions simultaneously.
  • Unlike many lizards, chameleons can’t regrow their tails.

Interesting Facts about Camel

  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Their eyes have three eyelids and two rows of eyelashes that prevent sand to enter their eyes

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • They can fly at the altitude of 6000 feet
  • Pigeons can fly at the speed of 50 to 60 miles per hour.
  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
